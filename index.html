Financial Model for Pacific Rice Farm & Mills

In this project, I developed a comprehensive financial model for Pacific Rice Farm & Mills, incorporating general and specific assumptions, including revenue, COGS, CAPEX, and OPEX. The model features detailed financial statements, in-depth financial analysis, and company valuation. A dynamic financial dashboard visualizes key performance indicators such as average revenue, gross profit, profit after tax, overall performance, OPEX breakdown, and profitability ratios. Additionally, the model supports scenario analysis, evaluating base, best, and worst-case scenarios to guide strategic planning.

Financial Model for SKIN TOP COMPANY

I developed a comprehensive financial model for “SKIN TOP”, a start-up company preparing to launch a new business unit focused on facial care products. The Financial Model is to support the company's funding efforts. The project involved forecasting revenue, expenses, and cash flows for the next five years for a product line consisting of face wash, moisturizer, and face scrub, each available in male and female versions. I analyzed various factors including customer acquisition costs, churn rates, pricing strategies, and growth projections.
